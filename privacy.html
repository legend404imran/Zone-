Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Information We Collect

When you use FF Paid Zone Push, we may collect:

  • Your Free Fire UID and player name, submitted through the order form.
  • Payment screenshots you share with us over WhatsApp to confirm your booking deposit.
  • Messages you send through AI Support, along with basic technical details such as your browser, the page you were on, and a randomly generated visitor identifier used only to tell repeat visits apart.
  • Any details you choose to share with us directly on WhatsApp.

How We Use It

We use this information only to confirm bookings, provide support, improve the accuracy of AI Support's answers, and resolve disputes about a specific order. We do not sell your information.

Support Chat Logs

Questions sent to AI Support are logged so we can review them and improve our answers over time. This happens automatically in the background and does not interrupt your chat.

Third-Party Services

We use WhatsApp to confirm bookings and payments, and a messaging tool to receive notifications of new support questions. These providers process data under their own privacy terms.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure, but no online service can guarantee absolute security. Avoid sharing sensitive financial details (like full card numbers) with us \u2014 we only ever need a payment screenshot and your UID.

Your Rights

You can ask us to delete your order history or chat logs at any time by messaging us on WhatsApp.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out on WhatsApp from our Contact page.
